 <span data-ttu-id="90046-138">**Verdächtige Domäne-Paare** enthalten:</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="90046-138">**Suspicious domain pairs** include:</span></span> 
  
- <span data-ttu-id="90046-p111">**Vertrauenswürdige Spoofing**. - <span data-ttu-id="90046-p112">**Moderater Confidence Spoofing**. This bucket has a greater chance of containing false positives (FPs) than the high-confidence spoof bucket.</span></span> 
    
 <span data-ttu-id="90046-p113">**Nicht-verdächtigen Domäne-Paare** enthalten **Spoofing wiederhergestellt**. As a result of this, Office 365 rescued the mail on your behalf and no anti-spoofing action was taken on the mail.</span></span>
